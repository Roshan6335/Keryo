Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Short version: We collect only what's necessary to run the app, never sell your data, and you can delete everything anytime.

1. Who We Are

Keryo AI is an independent AI assistant project built by Roshan. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

2. What We Collect

When you use Keryo AI, we may collect:

  • Account info — If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google's OAuth service.
  • Chat messages — Conversations you have with Keryo AI are stored so you can access them later.
  • Usage data — Basic technical info like your browser type and pages visited, collected anonymously to improve the app.
  • Settings & preferences — Your theme, language, and model preferences are stored locally in your browser.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any sensitive personal data.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the Keryo AI service
  • To save and display your chat history
  • To remember your preferences and settings
  • To respond to your messages through our AI engine

We do not use your data to train AI models, serve ads, or sell to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Keryo AI uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Google Sign-In — For authentication. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenRouter — To route AI requests. Your messages are sent to AI model providers via OpenRouter.
  • Supabase — For database storage of your chats and account info.
  • Apify — For web search functionality when you request live search results.
  • Pollinations.ai / Puter.js — For AI image generation when you request images.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We choose providers that prioritize data privacy.

5. Data Retention

Your chat history is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete individual chats or clear all chats at any time from within the app. If you sign out, locally stored preferences are cleared from your browser automatically.

6. Guest Mode

If you use Keryo AI as a guest (without signing in), your conversations are stored only in your browser's local storage and are not synced to our servers. Guest mode does not create an account and saves less data than a signed-in session. Your guest data is lost when you clear your browser data.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we have about you
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Use the app in guest mode without providing personal information
  • Opt out of any non-essential data collection

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: hello@keryo.ai

8. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure database storage through Supabase. However, no internet service is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Keryo AI is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
